Frequently asked questions
Paste the video URL and select a reason.
What is the EFED Streaming Hub?
A Netflix-style platform for CAW eFed wrestling content.
Is it free to use?
Yes — completely free. No subscriptions, no ads, no paywalls.
Can anyone post videos?
Any registered member can submit videos. All submissions are reviewed before going live.
Does watching here count toward YouTube views?
Yes! Videos use the official YouTube embed player.
How do I get an invite code?
Each member receives codes when they join. You can also request one from the sign-in page.
How do I report a video or comment?
On any video page tap the 3-dot menu to report.
Is my account information private?
Yes. Your email and password are never publicly visible.
