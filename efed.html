FAQ
What is the EFED Streaming Hub?
A Netflix-style platform for CAW eFed wrestling content — a central home base where fans and creators discover, watch, and share shows from various promotions.
Is it free to use?
Yes — completely free. No subscriptions, no ads, no paywalls. Sign up with an invite code and you're in.
Can anyone post videos?
Any registered member can submit videos. All submissions are reviewed to ensure they're genuine eFed wrestling content before going live.
Is there a limit to how many videos I can post?
No hard limit — but only real eFed show content. Spam or irrelevant submissions may result in account review.
Does watching here count toward YouTube views?
Yes! Videos use the official YouTube embed player, so every view on the Hub counts toward the creator's YouTube view count.
How do I get an invite code?
Each member receives invite codes when they join. You can also request one from the sign-in page.
How do I report a video or comment?
On any video page tap the 3-dot menu to report. For comments, use the flag icon. All reports go to the admin.
Is my account information private?
Yes. Your email and password are never publicly visible. Only your display name and Hub activity are visible to other members.
