What is the EFED Streaming Hub?
The EFED Streaming Hub is a Netflix-style platform dedicated to CAW eFed wrestling content. It's a central home base where fans and creators can discover, watch, and share shows from various eFed promotions.
Is it free to use?
Yes — completely free. No subscriptions, no ads, no paywalls. Just sign up with an invite code and you're in.
Can anyone post videos?
Any registered member can submit videos. All submissions are reviewed to make sure they're genuine eFed wrestling content before going live.
Is there a limit to how many videos I can post?
There's no hard limit — but we ask that you only submit real eFed show content. Spam or irrelevant submissions may result in your account being reviewed.
Does watching here count toward YouTube views?
Yes! Videos are embedded using the official YouTube player, so every view on the Hub counts toward the creator's YouTube view count.
How do I get an invite code?
Each member receives invite codes when they join. You can also request a code from the sign-in page if you don't have one yet.
How do I report a video or comment?
On any video page, use the flag button in the action bar to report a video. For comments, use the flag icon next to the comment. All reports are reviewed by the admin.
Is my account information private?
Yes. Your email and password are never publicly visible. Only your display name and activity on the Hub (likes, comments, posts) are visible to other members.
